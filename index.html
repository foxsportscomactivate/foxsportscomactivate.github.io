Simple and Easy Steps to Activate Fox now on Fire TV

  1. Turn on your Fire TV and connect it to the internet.
  2. Search for "Fox Now" in the Fire TV search bar.
  3. Select the Fox Now app from the search results.
  4. Click "Get" to download and install the app.
  5. Open the Fox Now app from the "Your Apps & Channels" section.
  6. Select "Activate" or "Sign In" on the screen.
  7. Note the activation code that appears on your Fire TV screen.
  8. Go to the website foxsports.com/activate on a computer or phone.
  9. Enter the activation code on the website.
  10. Sign in to your Fox account to complete the activation.
